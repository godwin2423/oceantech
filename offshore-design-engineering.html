Offshore Design & Engineering

Ocean Tech Solutions capability ranges from the concept development to detailed design of Jackup rig, Jackup barge (liftboat), offshore platform, through the design and installation aids. We have strong in-house team with experience and capabilities to complete the most challenging projects; Our range of services includes the following;

Structure engineering;

  • Finite element analysis of structures to meet the classification society rules ABS,DNV, BV,LR,IRS and engineering construction codes like AISC, API-RP2a, API 2C, IMO, AWS, CAP-437, ICAO& SOLAS.
  • Finite element analysis for both in-place and vessel motion cases.
  • Analysis and design of offshore platform including jacket, topside and accommodation module etc.
  • Jackup rig conversion and modifications.
  • Fatigue analysis
  • Modifications of existing structure for the new arrangements.
  • General arrangement and As-built preparation.
  • Offshore/Onshore survey
  • Landing craft design and analysis
  • Lifting analysis
  • Seafastening and grillage design
  • Installation aids

Piping engineering;

  • Piping engineering and design drafting
  • Pipe stress analysis
  • P&ID preparation
  • Material specifications and equipment selection
  • General arrangement, Isometric, spool creation and material take off.

Mechanical and HVAC;

  • Perform engineering design calculations and provide technical solutions for mechanical and HVAC system.
  • Duct sizing calculation
  • Review vendor quotations, vendor drawings and client documents.
  • Prepare test procedures for systems

Electrical engineering;

  • Electrical engineering and design drafting
  • Instrumentation engineering and design drafting
  • Single line diagram preparations
